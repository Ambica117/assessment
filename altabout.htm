We are delighted to invite you to our annual Navaratri celebrations, a vibrant and spiritually enriching event that promises an unforgettable evening filled with joy, devotion, and divine dance.

Date:

Event Highlights:

1. Garba and Dandiya Raas Dance:

Get ready to sway to the rhythm of traditional Garba and Dandiya Raas dances. Our skilled musicians and energetic dance performances will fill the night with infectious energy and enthusiasm.

2. Pooja and Aarti:

Participate in the auspicious Navaratri pooja and aarti, invoking the blessings of Goddess Durga. Experience the serenity of the prayer ritual and feel the divine presence surrounding you.

3. Cultural Performances:

Enjoy mesmerizing cultural performances by talented artists, showcasing the rich heritage and traditions of our culture. From classical dance to folk music, the evening promises a variety of entertaining acts.

4. Delicious Cuisine:

Indulge your taste buds in a sumptuous spread of traditional Navaratri delicacies. Our culinary experts have prepared a menu that captures the essence of authentic flavors, ensuring a delightful culinary experience.

5. Traditional Attire:

We encourage you to wear your finest traditional attire, adding to the colorful and festive ambiance of the event. Embrace the spirit of Navaratri by adorning yourself in vibrant colors and elegant ethnic wear.
